Project Introduction-----

This is a simple Rube Goldberg machine consisting of static and dynamic bodies.It is basically the movement of balls to finally kick the pin on the ground off

This includes various joints like revolute joint, pulley joint,etc.. .It has elements like conveyor belts, pullyes, dominos, balls and a pin.The final simulation looks as here
